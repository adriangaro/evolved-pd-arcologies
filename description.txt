[h1]Changes and Additions[/h1]
[list]
[*] Applies the arcology cost changes from Stellaris Evolved to the arcology decisions added by PD - More Arcologies.
[*] Use PD planet entities for some of the Evolved ecumenopolis specializations.
[*] Add support for Evolved civics to capital arcologies.
[*] Allow capital arcologies on elysiums and arcology habitats.
[/list]

[h1]Motivation[/h1]
Stellaris Evolved has already internal support for Planetary Diversity, however some edge cases were left out, one being new arcologies decisions.

[h1]Mod Load Order[/h1]
[list]
[*] Planetary Diversity and submods
[*] Stellaris Evolved - Alpha
[*] Stellaris Evolved + PD More Arcologies
[/list]
